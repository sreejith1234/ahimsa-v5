Advanced Health Information Management and Social Analytics. (AHIMSA)

"We are an inter disciplinary team of professionals
working together to:"

Simplify a clinician's life at the hospital

by ensuring that key decision making information is available at all times

Enable follow up and tracking of every patient

data originates from his / her residence (via community nurse visit) and ensures further followup and medical compliance (post procedure / advice)

Develop AI algorithms to
facilitate:

  • Predictive analytics for managing patient throughput
  • Measure procedure / device efficacies
  • Study patient demographics vs outcomes
  • Mortality (KM curves) analysis and population health studies

Derive "Social Analytics"

  • Community based, patient centric, clinician driven
  • Patient health and engagement as a central core
  • Data owned by the patient, consent given to share on a need basis
  • Real time communication with community nurse and care coordinators
  • Critical health parameters can be entered, monitored and viewed on a daily basis

Patient Referral and Engagement Platform (PREP)
  • Captures patient data right from referral until discharge, including previous cardiac surgeries, patient medical history, risk profiles and frailty scores.
  • Personnel from key feeder hospitals (cardiologists / admin) can view patient data originating from their region.
  • PREP ensures that NO patient gets lost in transit or while on "Waiting" or "OnHold" lists.
  • Each patient transition point is tied to a stakeholder responsible for action.
